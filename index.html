Books of Inspiration

The books I have listed below include links to author information and quotes that inspired me to trust myself and cope with the loss of my first and greatest love, Florence. A part of me died when I lost Florence. The best part of me was, and still is Her. My experience in love revealed challenging decisions that I made during a time of overwhelming grief and despair. I saw something that said, "the greatest loss is what dies inside of us while we live"; my loss was sudden, unexpected, and has left a bottomless pit of emptiness in my heart, and it is forever burned in my memory. My lesson in love is God's greatest gift to me; and I am forever changed. I thank God, the moon, and the stars for what heartbreak has taught me. I know My Florence is safe, healthy, and happy. There is not a day that goes by where I don't think of Florence or that I am reminded of Her. I don't know everything but, I know a lot more now.
